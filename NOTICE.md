WEINCE repository notice
========================

This repository contains WEINCE modifications, reproduction scripts, analysis notebooks, and documentation for "When Softmax Fails at the Top: Extreme-Value Corrections for InfoNCE".

The codebase is derived from the PyTorch SimCLR implementation by Janne Spijkervet. The original SimCLR project was released under the MIT License, and the original copyright notice is retained in `LICENSE`.

New WEINCE-specific code, notebooks, figures, and documentation are copyright 2026 Melihcan Erol, Suat Evren, Oktay Ozel, Alexander Morgan, Jongha Jon Ryu, and Lizhong Zheng, and are also released under the MIT License.
